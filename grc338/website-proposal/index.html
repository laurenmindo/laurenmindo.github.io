Introduction

Lauren's Closet

Lauren's Closet is a clothing boutique in downtown San Luis Obispo. The shop is full of cute clothes that are all inspired by Lauren's closet. Our main goal is to have our customers walk away from our store with a bag full of clothes that make them feel confident and beautiful.

Target audience

The people who visit this site are female college students and young adults. When you are in your late teens and early 20's, it can be an awkward time to buy clothes. You don't want to dresss like a high schooler but you don't need to be wearing professional clothes 24/7. That being said, you basically just want something cute and comfortable to wear.

Our customers are looking to buy stylish clothes for any event. No matter the occasion, whether you are attending a wedding, hanging out with friends around town, or working out, you are sure to find something nice to wear.
