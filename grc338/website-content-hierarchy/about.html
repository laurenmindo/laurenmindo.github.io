About Us

Founder of The Acai Cafe, Lauren, was a Cal Poly graduate who went on to get her Master's degee in Marketing at Vanderbilt.

Acai, along with many of our other ingredients are considered super foods. Superfoods are nutritionally dense foods that provide a powerful sources of antioxidants, healthy fats, vitamins, minerals and other health promoting properties.

After living in Nashville for a year, she realized she missed the Acai bowls and smoothies from California. So, she decided to open up her very own Acai Cafe, bringing a little taste of California to Nashville.
